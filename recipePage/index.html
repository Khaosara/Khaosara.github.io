Fried Plantain with scrambled eggs!

Prep: 15 minutes           Cook time: 20 minutes          Total time: 35 minutes

    List of ingredients

  • 1/4 white onion finely chopped
  • 1/2 Roma Tomato
  • 1 tbsp of Coconut oil or any cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 ripe plaintain chopped
  • 1 cup of spinach finely
  • 1 cup of kale finelly chopped
  • 1 can of tuna (optional)

Plaintain portion

  1. Cut the ends of the plaintain. Keep the skin on (alternatively you can keep the skin off but keep it mind it will boil quicker- its up to personal perference).
  2. Cut the plaintain to your preference. (However be mindful that if you cut it smaller it will cook, fry, boil, or bake faster).
  3. In a pot, fill it up half way with water. Put the stovetop on medium heat, & boil water.
  4. While the water is boiling, cut the plaintain to your preference. However be mindful that if you cut it smaller it will cook, fry, boil, or bake faster.
  5. Once it boils add in the plaintain.
  6. Boil for 20 minutes or till tender soft (pale yellow in color.

Scrambled portion

  1. In a bowl crack the 3 eggs add salt and pepper then mix
  2. On a cutting board or regular plate finely chop the onion, tomatoes, spinach, and kale.
  3. In a frying pan, heat the pan then add oil.
  4. Once the pan is hot enough, sautee the onion and tomatoes till tender and translucent in colour.
  5. Add the kale and spinach.
  6. Add the mixed eggs with tuna (optional) to the pan then cook and mix till cooked (or even omelette or poached- it's really personal perference).
